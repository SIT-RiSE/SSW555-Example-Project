Your SSW555 Starter Pack

Pick one standalone app to launch your project. Each module already lives in its own folder, so you can remove the rest without breaking anything.

Reminder: This isn’t a bundled multi-function platform. Choose one module, plug it into your work, then delete the extras from templates/apps/ and the matching routes in app.py.

Why everything is split up

Each app ships in its own folder (templates/apps/<app-name>) with shared layout assets kept in templates/base.html and static/css.

Removing a module is as simple as deleting its template folder and any related database models or routes. Nothing else depends on it.

Use this structure as a teaching tool or a lightweight launchpad for SSW555 deliverables.
